Archax, the UK/EU-regulated digital asset platform, today announced real-time streaming cash flows for tokenized securities on Hedera, the trusted public network for building fast, secure, and compliant decentralized applications. This capability enables interest payments to be distributed on a near second-by-second basis directly to investors' wallets using Circle's USDC stablecoin on Hedera.
